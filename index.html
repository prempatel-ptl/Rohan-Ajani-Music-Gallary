About Me

Rohan Ajani

Rohan Ajani is a Mumbai-based musician, composer, and performer with over a decade of experience in classical and contemporary music. His work bridges the gap between traditional Indian classical forms and modern acoustic expression.

Having performed at venues across India — from intimate recitals in Pune to large stage concerts in Delhi and Chennai — Rohan brings a distinctive voice and a deep respect for the craft to every performance.
